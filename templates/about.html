About Us

Matoshree Hospital is a beacon of excellence in healthcare, committed to providing compassionate and cutting-edge medical services. With a legacy of trust and innovation, we prioritize patient well-being above all.

  • 24/7 Emergency Care: Always ready to serve in critical situations.
  • Specialized Departments: Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, and more.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Equipped with modern medical technology.
  • Expert Medical Team: Highly qualified doctors and dedicated staff.
  • Holistic Healthcare: Personalized patient care and wellness programs.

"Your Health, Our Mission" drives us to ensure every patient receives the highest quality care in a supportive and comfortable environment. Experience healthcare with a human touch at Matoshree Hospital.
