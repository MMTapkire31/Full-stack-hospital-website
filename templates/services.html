24/7 Emergency Care

Our 24/7 Emergency Care department is equipped to handle any medical crisis with speed and expertise. Our emergency response team includes experienced physicians, nurses, and support staff who are trained to provide immediate care for critical conditions.

Key Features:
Round-the-clock emergency services
Advanced life support ambulances
Trauma care specialists
Critical care units
Seamless transition to inpatient care

Our emergency department is designed to provide efficient care with minimal waiting times. We prioritize cases based on severity and ensure that every patient receives the attention they need without unnecessary delay.

For medical emergencies, please call our emergency hotline at +01 1234567890 or proceed directly to our emergency department.

First Aid & Trauma Care

Our First Aid & Trauma Care services provide immediate response to injuries and medical emergencies. Our trauma team is trained to handle a wide range of situations from minor injuries to major trauma cases.

Our Capabilities:
Immediate assessment and treatment of injuries
Advanced trauma life support
Wound care and management
Fracture stabilization
Burn treatment

Our trauma center is equipped with specialized equipment and staffed by professionals who are experts in emergency medicine. We work closely with emergency services to ensure seamless transition of care for patients arriving by ambulance.

We also offer first aid training programs for community members, schools, and organizations to promote safety awareness and preparedness.

Patient-Centered Care

At Matoshree Hospital, our Patient-Centered Care approach puts you at the center of everything we do. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, and involving you in all decisions about your healthcare.

Our Approach Includes:
Personalized treatment plans
Shared decision-making
Cultural sensitivity and respect
Family involvement in care
Continuity of care coordination

Our healthcare team takes the time to listen to your concerns, answer your questions, and explain treatment options in clear, understandable terms. We respect your values, preferences, and needs in developing and implementing your treatment plan.
